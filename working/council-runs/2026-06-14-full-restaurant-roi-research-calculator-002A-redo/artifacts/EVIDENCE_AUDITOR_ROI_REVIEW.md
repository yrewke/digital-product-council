# Evidence Auditor ROI Review

## Overall Disposition

The run supports an internal scenario calculator and a future owner-data public diagnostic. It does not support a predictive UAE ROI benchmark calculator.

## Unsupported or Restricted Claims

- Do not claim direct ordering is cheaper than aggregators without actual statements and all direct costs.
- Do not claim expected shifted-order, incremental-order, AOV, time-saving, mistake-reduction, or catering-win rates.
- Do not call archetypes proven best fits.
- Do not recommend pickup, outsourced, hybrid, WhatsApp, Telegram, bots, POS, or AI without restaurant-specific thresholds.
- Do not monetize trust, SEO, credibility, customer data, resilience, or brand legitimacy automatically.

## Safe Numbers

- Stripe UAE standard pricing can be shown as a labeled, editable provider reference.
- Telegram's normal Bot API charge can be shown as zero only with development/hosting/support caveats.
- Marketplace, rider, fulfilment, AOV, and competitor numbers are context or sensitivity evidence, not customer defaults.

## Default Policy

- Required owner inputs: orders, AOV, margin, actual deductions, fulfilment, promotion, setup, recurring costs.
- Default to zero or blank: shifted orders, incremental orders, AOV uplift, time savings, error reduction, catering wins.
- Illustrative sensitivity values must be separated and labeled “not UAE benchmark.”

## Missing-Cost Requirements

Include promotion/traffic generation, setup/content/training, refunds/chargebacks, full delivery failure cost, full rider employer/fleet cost, pickup congestion/no-shows, vendor/integration costs, provider support, and cannibalization.

## Release Decision

Keep AED/payback output internal or evidence-gated. A public quick diagnostic may identify likely value paths, data gaps, risks, and recommended next measurement without presenting a likely ROI forecast.
