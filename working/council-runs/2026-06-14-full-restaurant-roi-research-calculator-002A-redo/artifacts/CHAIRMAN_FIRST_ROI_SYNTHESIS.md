# Chairman First ROI Synthesis

## Emerging Direction

Build an **owner-data ROI scenario calculator**, not a UAE benchmark predictor. Its job is to reveal whether a specific restaurant has a credible owned-channel case, which value path carries it, which costs can destroy it, and whether the correct recommendation is buy, test, use an alternative, or do nothing.

## Strongest ROI Logic

1. **Actual repeat orders shifted from a high-deduction channel**, after subtracting direct payment, promotion, fulfilment, support, and failure costs.
2. **Pickup or delivery-zone leakage correction**, where current costs are known.
3. **Measured manual-order leakage**, including missed orders, mistakes, and staff handling.
4. **High-ticket catering/family-tray enquiry capture**, where enquiries, wins, ticket, and margin are tracked.
5. **Incremental direct orders**, only after source tracking proves incrementality.

Trust, SEO, brand legitimacy, customer data, resilience, and optionality are real strategic values but should not receive automatic AED values.

## Calculator Structure

- Require owner records for core economics.
- Default positive causal assumptions to zero or blank.
- Put illustrative scenarios in a separate sensitivity panel labeled as assumptions.
- Keep shifted, incremental, operational, fulfilment, and lead modules separate.
- Prevent double counting.
- Compare against best alternative and doing nothing.
- Show cash effect, capacity released, qualitative strategic score, missing-data warning, and no-break-even result.

## Archetype Direction

Recruit early tests from WhatsApp-heavy, family-tray/catering-led, aggregator-heavy, pickup-capable, and weak-website-with-demand restaurants. These are hypotheses, not proven segment rankings. Require usable records and an accountable operator.

## Channel and Fulfilment Direction

Start with the smallest reversible stack that solves the measured problem. Website/order page plus WhatsApp handoff and Telegram receiver is a practical test candidate, not a universal default. Pickup or existing fulfilment should be tested before new fleet or complex integrations. Bots, AI, POS, and delivery APIs require separate ROI and support gates.

## First Decision

The future public calculator should have two modes:

1. **Quick diagnostic:** asks a small number of owner questions and returns data-quality/risk guidance, not a promised AED result.
2. **Evidence-based model:** uses restaurant-entered statements, logs, costs, and explicit assumptions to calculate scenarios.

The current calculator remains internal-first until the evidence audit and pilot thresholds are applied.
