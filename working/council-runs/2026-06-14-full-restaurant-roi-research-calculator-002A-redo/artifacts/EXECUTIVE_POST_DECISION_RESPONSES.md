# Executive Post-Decision Responses

## Owner Economist

**Right:** The synthesis makes payback restaurant-specific and permits “do not buy.”  
**Missed:** Owner cash timing, settlement delay, and upfront downside deserve stronger visibility.  
**Overstated:** The availability of usable owner records.  
**Understated:** Clear responsibility boundaries as a trust driver.  
**Careful number:** Shifted-order rate.  
**Must-have variable:** Total upfront and recurring cash obligations.  
**Change:** Show downside cash exposure before upside.

## Operator

**Right:** Small reversible stack and measured leakage.  
**Missed:** Peak-hour acknowledgement and exception closure gates.  
**Overstated:** Pickup simplicity.  
**Understated:** Complaint/refund traceability.  
**Careful number:** Minutes saved per order.  
**Must-have variable:** Peak handling/acknowledgement failure.  
**Change:** Recommendations require a named shift owner and peak test.

## Marketing and Trust Analyst

**Right:** Trust is not automatically monetized.  
**Missed:** Existing attention can be diagnosed before ordering is added.  
**Overstated:** Direct ordering as the necessary first intervention.  
**Understated:** Clear menu/enquiry destination.  
**Careful number:** Traffic-to-order conversion.  
**Must-have variable:** Source-specific visits/actions.  
**Change:** Allow a presence/enquiry-only recommendation.

## Direct-Order Economics Analyst

**Right:** Shifted/incremental separation and double-count prevention.  
**Missed:** Contribution margin definitions are often misunderstood.  
**Overstated:** Any benchmark-derived high scenario.  
**Understated:** Direct discounts and fulfilment deltas.  
**Careful number:** Marketplace deductions.  
**Must-have variable:** Contribution after all variable costs.  
**Change:** Require actual statement and margin definition before payback.

## Delivery and Fulfilment Analyst

**Right:** Avoid new fleet before data.  
**Missed:** Waiting, return, redelivery, parking, toll, and peak charges.  
**Overstated:** Outsourced/hybrid as generally safer.  
**Understated:** Zone and minimum-order rules.  
**Careful number:** Cost per delivery.  
**Must-have variable:** Full failed-delivery cost.  
**Change:** Compare delivery models only after complete cost entry.

## Channel Analyst

**Right:** Bots and AI require separate gates.  
**Missed:** Customer-channel adoption differs by restaurant audience.  
**Overstated:** Website + WhatsApp + Telegram as a practical candidate everywhere.  
**Understated:** Channel consolidation and human handoff.  
**Careful number:** Bot containment/conversion.  
**Must-have variable:** Current channel volume.  
**Change:** Recommend no new channel when existing one is adequate.

## Competitor Analyst

**Right:** Best-alternative comparison.  
**Missed:** Existing POS/SaaS can be operationally superior despite higher advertised price.  
**Overstated:** Product-owned implementation as default.  
**Understated:** Doing nothing.  
**Careful number:** Competitor advertised prices.  
**Must-have variable:** Full alternative cost and burden.  
**Change:** Explicit alternative recommendation is mandatory.

## Developer Cost Analyst

**Right:** Internal-first and separate support gates.  
**Missed:** Provider contribution should influence whether a configuration is offered at all.  
**Overstated:** Availability of bounded self-service.  
**Understated:** One-developer continuity.  
**Careful number:** Support hours.  
**Must-have variable:** Provider hours/account/month.  
**Change:** Add provider viability gate to Run 2B.

## Evidence Auditor

**Right:** Zero/blank defaults and internal-first posture.  
**Missed:** Claim-level evidence ledger must be completed.  
**Overstated:** Archetype priorities remain too strong.  
**Understated:** Missing-cost warnings.  
**Careful number:** Every positive causal input.  
**Must-have variable:** Provenance/data-quality status.  
**Change:** Public quick diagnostic must avoid AED output without records.
