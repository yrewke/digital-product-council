# ROI Owner Questionnaire

## Orders and Economics

1. How many total orders do you complete in a normal month, by dine-in, pickup, own delivery, aggregator, WhatsApp, phone, and social DM?
2. What is the average order value by channel?
3. What is the approximate contribution margin after food and packaging costs?
4. What exact deductions appear on recent aggregator statements, including commission, delivery, payment, promotion, advertising, and other fees?
5. Which orders are likely repeat customers versus first-time discovery?

## Current Direct and Manual Orders

6. How many WhatsApp and phone orders arrive monthly?
7. How many minutes does staff spend clarifying and entering each manual order?
8. How often are messages missed, totals wrong, addresses incomplete, items unavailable, or orders duplicated?
9. What does a typical mistake, refund, remake, or lost order cost?
10. Who owns the order queue during normal and peak hours?

## Pickup and Delivery

11. What percentage of orders are pickup and delivery?
12. Do you already have a driver/rider? What is the full monthly cost including salary, visa/accommodation if applicable, bike, fuel, insurance, maintenance, and management?
13. What is your normal radius, delivery fee, minimum order, and average delivery cost?
14. Do you use outsourced delivery? What are the actual per-order, cancellation, waiting, return, and peak charges?
15. What percentage of deliveries fail, arrive late, trigger complaints, or require refunds?

## Website, Google, Social, and Leads

16. Do you have a website? Does it show a current menu and accept orders?
17. How many monthly visits, Google actions, Instagram profile visits/link clicks, calls, DMs, and QR scans do you receive?
18. How many catering, family-tray, office, event, reservation, or group-order enquiries arrive monthly?
19. What is the average ticket, win rate, and contribution margin for those enquiries?
20. What customer question or trust concern most often prevents an order?

## Repeat Customers and Promotions

21. What share of customers reorder, and how is that measured?
22. Do you have permission and a process to contact customers again?
23. What discounts or promotions do you fund by channel?
24. Which offer has produced measured profitable repeat demand?

## Systems and Responsibility

25. Which POS is used? Is online order injection or menu sync available, and at what fee?
26. Which payment methods are needed? What are actual gateway fees and dispute/refund costs?
27. Who will keep menu prices, photos, availability, hours, zones, and fees current?
28. Who handles complaints, refunds, failed delivery, and outages?
29. Are you interested in website ordering, WhatsApp handoff, deterministic WhatsApp bot, Telegram receiver/bot, or a bounded AI assistant?
30. What monthly result would make the system worthwhile, and what downside would make you stop?

## Data Request

Ask for one representative month of aggregator statements, POS/channel order counts, driver/outsourced-delivery costs, refunds/complaints, and website/social analytics where available. Calculate from records before relying on recall.
