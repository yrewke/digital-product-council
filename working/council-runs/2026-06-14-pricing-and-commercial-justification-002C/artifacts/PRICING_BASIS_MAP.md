# Pricing Basis Map

| Basis | Role in decision | Safe use | Unsafe use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Restaurant ROI ceiling | Tests affordability and payback sensitivity | Owner-specific conservative scenario | Guaranteed savings or universal payback |
| Competitor ceiling | Shows alternatives and objection context | Compare jobs/responsibilities | Treat unlike offers as equivalent |
| Developer cost floor | Protects delivery and continuity | Hours x blended rate plus risk | Present assumptions as measured facts |
| Support/responsibility load | Determines recurring requirement | Explicit allowance and overage | Unlimited support |
| Third-party costs | Determines pass-through budget | Direct pay or transparent pass-through | Hidden markup/cost |
| Hosting mode | Changes ownership and operational burden | Different setup/recurring structure | Imply zero responsibility |
| Cashflow/urgency | Supports deposit and launch offer | Bounded launch discount | Permanent discounting |
| Market-entry strategy | Buys validation participation | Limited named launch cohort | Public low-price anchor |
| Sustainability | Protects one-developer continuity | Margin/risk buffer and gates | Underprice high-variance work |
| Trust/simplicity | Reduces sales friction | Three base choices, finite modules | Feature-by-feature public menu |

## Initial Cost Formula

`Minimum setup fee = estimated productive hours x AED 175 + risk/collection buffer`

`Managed monthly fee = included support value + monitoring/backup/vendor responsibility + continuity reserve`

These are council assumptions until delivery and support time are measured.
