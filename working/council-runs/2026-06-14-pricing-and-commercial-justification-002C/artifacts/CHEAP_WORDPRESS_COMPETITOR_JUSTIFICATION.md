# Cheap WordPress Competitor Justification

## Internal Strategic Version

An AED 300 WordPress offer is an unsourced objection hypothesis, not a verified UAE benchmark. It may be the right answer when the buyer needs only a static business card, basic menu, map, contact details, or WhatsApp button.

It is not equivalent when the buyer requires structured menu/cart orders, order records, Telegram notification, source tracking, accountable workflow, training, support boundaries, fallback, export, and responsibility handling.

Do not offer a separate brochure-only response. Recommend the cheaper provider when brochure-only is the real job. Tier 1 is justified only where the owned destination plus structured ordering job is observed.

## Owner-Facing Draft

We are not pricing only a page. We are pricing restaurant-specific ordering setup and clearly bounded responsibility. If you only need a basic website with your menu and contact details, a cheaper WordPress site may be the better choice. Our offer is for restaurants that need structured orders, defined setup, training, support, and an upgrade path.
