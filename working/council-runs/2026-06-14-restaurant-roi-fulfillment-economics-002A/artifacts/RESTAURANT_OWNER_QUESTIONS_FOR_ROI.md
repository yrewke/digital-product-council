# Restaurant Owner Questions for ROI

Ask these in sales calls to calculate fulfilment ROI:

1. How many total orders do you get per month?
2. How many are delivery orders?
3. How many are pickup orders?
4. What is your average order value?
5. What is your gross margin or food cost percentage?
6. What share of orders comes from aggregators today?
7. What do you currently pay in aggregator commissions and fees?
8. How do you handle delivery today?
9. Do you have an existing driver or staff member who already delivers?
10. If yes, what does that driver cost per month?
11. If no, would you ever hire a rider or buy/lease a motorcycle?
12. What is your current WhatsApp order volume?
13. What is your phone order volume?
14. How many order mistakes or corrections happen in a typical week?
15. How many complaints, refunds, or redeliveries do you see in a month?
16. Do you have a POS system?
17. Do you have a website or order page today?
18. How much traffic comes from Instagram, Google, QR codes, or in-store prompts?
19. What is your delivery radius?
20. Which zones are most expensive or slow to serve?
21. How often do customers repeat order?
22. Do customers ask to reorder by chat or link?
23. Would your team actually use WhatsApp or Telegram ordering?
24. Would you test a direct ordering page if aggregators stayed live?
25. What data would you need to trust the new channel?
26. What would make you stop using it?

## Minimum Data Needed to Model ROI

- Monthly orders
- Delivery versus pickup split
- Average order value
- Gross margin or food cost
- Current fulfilment method
- Delivery labor cost
- Error/refund rate
- Customer repeat behavior
- Traffic source mix
- Interest in direct ordering channels
