# Evidence Auditor Review

## Supported Claims

- Aggregators are a material discovery and commission baseline.
- Pickup is the lowest-burden fulfilment mode.
- Existing-driver delivery is preferable to hiring a new fleet when capacity already exists.
- WhatsApp/manual ordering is operationally fragile at higher volume.
- Hybrid fulfilment can balance control and burden.

## Unsupported or Weak Claims

- Any exact Dubai-wide break-even threshold.
- Any claim that direct ordering is universally cheaper than aggregators.
- Any claim that a rider or motorcycle is good without proving delivery density.
- Any claim that WhatsApp/manual ordering scales cleanly.

## Auditor Findings

- The verdict correctly avoids final pricing and sales copy.
- The verdict still relies on scenario assumptions for actual monthly demand, which is unavoidable in this run.
- The most important missing field data is restaurant monthly order mix and delivery radius.
