# Advisor Memo: Competitor and Alternatives Analyst

**Commercial question:** Under which fulfilment model does owned direct ordering create positive ROI versus alternatives?
**Verdict:** oppose
**Strongest finding:** WhatsApp/manual ordering and doing nothing are real competitors, and for very small restaurants they are often better than a structured owned channel.
**Evidence used:** Run 001 WhatsApp evidence, aggregator coexistence evidence, and customer control findings.
**Critical assumption:** The restaurant will not suddenly become much more disciplined after buying software.
**Tradeoff:** A structured website is more scalable, but a chat workflow can be lower-friction at the start.
**Disagreement expected:** Others will overestimate how much traffic a small restaurant can convert into paid direct orders.
**Evidence request:** Actual phone/WhatsApp order counts and the number of corrections per week.
