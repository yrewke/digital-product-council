# Advisor Memo: Market Economics Analyst

**Commercial question:** Under which fulfilment model does owned direct ordering create positive ROI versus alternatives?
**Verdict:** conditional
**Strongest finding:** The economics are driven by order volume, repeat rate, and whether direct orders actually shift commission-heavy orders rather than creating net-new low-value work.
**Evidence used:** Run 001 aggregator and repeat-behavior evidence; Run 002A break-even assumptions.
**Critical assumption:** Direct-channel traffic exists or can be created at low cost from existing owned touchpoints.
**Tradeoff:** High-margin restaurants can tolerate more delivery cost, but low-margin ones cannot.
**Disagreement expected:** The competitor analyst will argue that WhatsApp/manual is a stronger substitute than a website for some small restaurants.
**Evidence request:** Conversion rate by channel and repeat-order rate after first direct order.
