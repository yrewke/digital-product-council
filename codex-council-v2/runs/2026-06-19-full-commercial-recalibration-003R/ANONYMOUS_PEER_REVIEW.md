# Anonymous Peer Review

Twenty anonymous review packets were completed and merged through the V2 routing map. Main objections:

- ROI must output archetype/channel-specific results, not universal payback.
- Value map must separate immediate cash return from operational and strategic value.
- Delivery capacity must gate direct-delivery ROI.
- Pricing must show support caps, pass-throughs, exclusions, and pilot stop conditions.
- Low-readiness/high-need restaurants need a staged positive path.
- AI should be staged later unless volume, use case, and support budget justify it.
- Alternatives comparison must include both cheap purchase price and operational failure cost.

All five executives accepted the material review pressure in consolidated author responses.
