# Revised Product Category Model

## Core Products

1. Entry Direct Presence
   - Menu page, basic website/presence, Google/social links, enquiry or WhatsApp handoff.
   - For high-need low-readiness or low-volume restaurants.
   - Not sold as ROI product.

2. Ordering Page
   - Structured menu, pickup ordering, optional payment, WhatsApp handoff, basic analytics.
   - Smallest independently valuable direct-ordering product.

3. Website + Ordering
   - Brand site plus ordering, pickup/delivery settings, payment option, basic SEO, analytics.
   - Main early package for repeat-demand independent restaurants.

4. Direct Orders Dashboard
   - Order management, customer capture, basic CRM exports, campaigns/loyalty-lite, operational reporting.
   - For restaurants with enough volume and owner follow-through.

5. Direct Commerce
   - Multi-module direct channel with CRM/loyalty, analytics, catering, advanced delivery rules, branch logic, and optional integrations.
   - Custom scope for mature restaurants.

## Optional Modules

WhatsApp handoff, WhatsApp deterministic bot, Telegram bot, catering enquiries, loyalty/CRM, multilingual, QR ordering, delivery dispatch, analytics, payment integration, AI assistant, POS integration, delivery integration, multi-branch logic.

## Placement Rules

- Default only when broadly useful and supportable.
- Optional when need/cost/risk varies.
- Custom quote when third-party access, liability, delivery, POS, or AI usage can materially change cost.
