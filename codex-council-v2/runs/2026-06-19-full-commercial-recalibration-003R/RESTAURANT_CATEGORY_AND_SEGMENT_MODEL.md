# Restaurant Category and Segment Model

## Decision-Useful Archetypes

| Archetype | Need | Value | Readiness | Starting Product | Delivery Guidance |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Repeat local delivery restaurant | Marketplace dependence, repeat customers, margin pressure. | High if AOV/margin support incentives and delivery. | Medium if menu and ops are clean. | Website + Ordering or Direct Orders Dashboard. | Existing drivers or reliable 3PL; no owned rider until volume supports it. |
| Pickup-friendly quick service | Phone/WhatsApp friction, queue/order accuracy, repeat habit. | Medium/high through pickup, QR, repeat campaigns. | Often high. | Ordering Page + pickup + QR. | Pickup first; delivery later. |
| Catering/group-order candidate | Lost enquiries, manual quoting, no lead capture. | High but conversion uncertain. | Medium. | Menu + catering enquiry + CRM follow-up. | Delivery quoted separately; do not model as normal delivery. |
| Premium/specialty brand | Brand credibility, data ownership, loyalty, high AOV. | High strategic and medium-term measurable return. | Medium/high. | Website + Ordering + CRM/loyalty. | Delivery quality must match brand. |
| Low-volume new restaurant | Discoverability, no owned assets, limited cash. | Uncertain. | Low/medium. | Entry Direct Presence or Ordering Page. | Pickup/WhatsApp handoff first. |
| Multi-branch operator | Branch consistency, analytics, integrations. | High if operational backbone exists. | High but complex. | Direct Commerce custom scope. | Integration and dispatch custom quote. |
| Marketplace-only discovery kitchen | Demand exists mainly inside marketplace. | Low direct migration until brand demand exists. | Variable. | Hygiene/brand page first; no heavy system. | Keep marketplace; test inserts/QR only. |

## First Target

Prioritize owner-led restaurants with repeat demand, medium/high AOV, high marketplace dependence, and operational ability to prompt customers. Avoid restaurants whose only demand source is marketplace discovery unless the pilot is explicitly a brand-building experiment.
