# Chairman Provisional Verdict

Replace the old tier-first model with a staged hybrid model:

- Entry Direct Presence.
- Ordering Page.
- Website + Ordering.
- Direct Orders Dashboard.
- Direct Commerce.

Use optional modules for WhatsApp, Telegram, CRM/loyalty, analytics, catering, delivery dispatch, AI, POS, and delivery integrations. Use internal launch prices only, not public guaranteed pricing.

Early pilots should focus on repeat-demand, medium/high AOV, high marketplace dependence, owner-led restaurants where direct prompts and fulfillment discipline are realistic.
