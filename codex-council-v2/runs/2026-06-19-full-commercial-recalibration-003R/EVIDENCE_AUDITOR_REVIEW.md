# Evidence Auditor Review

## Result

Audit passed after 32 resolved fact-check records. One deterministic parser issue was resolved as a false positive: a sentence listing separate scenario ranges was not a margin calculation.

## Binding Audit Rules

- Every material number must carry a label.
- Verified external facts are limited to inspected official/vendor pages.
- NotebookLM outputs are internal synthesis unless backed by inspected external sources.
- Strategic benefits are not immediate AED ROI.
- Shifted marketplace orders are not incremental orders.
- Delivery cost and capacity must be modeled before direct-delivery benefit is counted.
- Payments, WhatsApp/SMS, AI usage, ad spend, third-party delivery, and POS subscriptions are pass-through or capped unless explicitly bundled.
- No public guaranteed ROI or marketplace replacement claim.

## Scoped Veto

Auditor raised V-0001 against the provisional verdict. It was resolved when the final verdict explicitly required number labels, support caps, pass-throughs, and pilot validation.
