# Pilot Validation Plan

## Pilot Goal

Replace planning assumptions with restaurant-specific evidence for migration, incentive cost, delivery feasibility, support burden, willingness to pay, and ROI mechanics.

## Pilot Candidates

3 to 5 restaurants across:

- Repeat local delivery.
- Pickup-friendly QSR.
- Catering/group-order candidate.
- Premium/specialty brand.

## Required Measurements

- Baseline marketplace orders by repeat/new customer if available.
- Eligible repeat marketplace order estimate.
- Direct order count by source.
- Shifted vs incremental classification.
- Incentive cost and redemption rate.
- Payment fees.
- Delivery cost, radius, SLA, failures, refunds.
- Order errors and staff handling time.
- Catering enquiries, qualified leads, closes, and order value.
- Support tickets and minutes.
- Owner/staff adherence.
- Customer switching reasons.

## Stop Conditions

- Restaurant cannot keep menu/order status accurate.
- Delivery failures materially damage customer experience.
- Support exceeds allowance by more than 2x for two consecutive weeks.
- Direct orders require incentives that consume the margin case.
- Owner will not prompt customers or share baseline data.

## Success Conditions

- Clear source tracking.
- Positive or explainable path to positive monthly net benefit.
- Support load inside package allowance or justified upsell.
- Delivery model operationally feasible.
- Restaurant agrees the direct channel solves a real problem even when ROI is not immediate.
