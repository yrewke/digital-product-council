# Revised Package, Tier, and Module Model

| Old Concept | New Product | Included | Optional | Excluded/Custom |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Tier 0 | Entry Direct Presence | Menu/presence, enquiry/WhatsApp link, basic analytics | Catering enquiry, QR | ROI guarantee, payments, delivery integration |
| Tier 1 | Ordering Page | Structured menu, pickup, order form/page, basic status, analytics | Payment, WhatsApp handoff, QR | POS, advanced delivery, AI |
| Tier 2 | Website + Ordering | Website, ordering, pickup/delivery settings, basic SEO, analytics | CRM-lite, loyalty, catering, payment | POS/delivery integration, multi-branch |
| Tier 2/3 | Direct Orders Dashboard | Orders dashboard, customer records, reports, campaign export | CRM/loyalty, dispatch rules, multilingual | Unlimited support, guaranteed delivery SLA |
| Tier 3 | Direct Commerce | Advanced direct channel and operational modules | AI, POS, delivery integration, multi-branch | Anything without scoped SOW |

## Standard Allowances

- Basic packages include capped support and normal hosting.
- Messaging, WhatsApp, SMS, payment fees, AI usage, ad spend, delivery fees, POS fees, and third-party subscriptions are pass-through unless explicitly bundled.
- Overage requires approval or pre-agreed rate.
