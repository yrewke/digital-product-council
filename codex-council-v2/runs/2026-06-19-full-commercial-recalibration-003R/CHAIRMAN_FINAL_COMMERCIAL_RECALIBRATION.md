# Chairman Final Commercial Recalibration

## Final Model

Use a staged hybrid commercial model with buyable core products, optional modules, custom-quote integrations, restaurant-specific ROI diagnostics, and delivery feasibility gates.

## Non-Negotiable Boundaries

- No marketplace replacement claim.
- No guaranteed ROI or universal payback claim.
- No universal POS or delivery integration promise.
- No claim that software solves missing physical delivery capacity.
- No hidden pass-through costs.
- No uncapped support in low monthly fees.

## Current Product Structure

Entry Direct Presence, Ordering Page, Website + Ordering, Direct Orders Dashboard, and Direct Commerce.

## Current Pricing Posture

Prices are internal launch/standard planning ranges. Public pricing waits for pilot evidence. All material numbers require number labels, support caps, pass-throughs, and pilot validation.

## First Pilot Segment

Owner-led restaurants with repeat demand, medium/high AOV, visible existing demand, marketplace dependence, and ability to prompt customers.

## Decision

This run supersedes the old tier-first commercial model. Future product development, sales diagnostics, ROI reports, pricing work, and pilot design should use this artifact pack as the current baseline.
