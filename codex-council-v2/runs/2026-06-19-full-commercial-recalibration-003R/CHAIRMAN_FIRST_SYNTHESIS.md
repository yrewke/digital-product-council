# Chairman First Synthesis

Adopt a staged hybrid commercial model. Target repeat-demand restaurants with measurable direct-order potential first, but separate need, value, and readiness. Retain marketplaces as discovery and overflow channels; do not promise replacement.

ROI must separate incremental orders, shifted marketplace orders, structured phone/WhatsApp orders, pickup, delivery, and catering. Product should use buyable tiers plus optional modules and custom-quote integrations. Pricing must triangulate value, affordability, alternatives, support burden, provider cost, and pilot risk.

Delivery capacity is a hard gate, not a software promise.
