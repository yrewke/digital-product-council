# Provider Cost And Allowance Model

**Assumptions:** AED 175 productive hour; setup hours T0 10-16, T1 20-32, T2 40-65; 15% buffer. These are unmeasured.

| Scope | Assumed setup hours | Inferred buffered floor | Primary risk | Commercial protection |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|
| T0 | 10-16 | AED 2,000-3,250 | Cleanup/missed notifications | Fixed allowance, named owner |
| T1 | 20-32 | AED 4,000-6,500 | Content/design creep | Template/revision limits |
| T2 | 40-65 | AED 8,000-13,100 | Workflow/training/incidents | Paid discovery, mandatory support |
| Bounded modules | Unknown | Quote | Dependency variance | Acceptance/data gate |
| Bots/payment/delivery/integrations | Unknown/high | Discovery/custom | Vendor/failure responsibility | No fixed price |

Measure acquisition, qualification, cleanup, setup, revisions, training, support, incidents, vendor coordination, collections, renewal, export, cancellation, and offboarding for every launch client.
