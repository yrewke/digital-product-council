# Run 003 Sales Deliverables Handoff

## Use

- Internal launch prices: T0 AED 2,900; T1 AED 5,900; T2 AED 12,000 after paid discovery.
- Internal standard hypotheses: T0 AED 3,600; T1 AED 7,500; T2 AED 15,000 after discovery.
- Managed monthly: AED 350 / 650 / 1,100.
- T2 discovery: internal AED 2,500 hypothesis.

## Say

- Aggregators remain discovery channels.
- Estimate ROI using owner data; no guarantee.
- Price setup, retained responsibility, vendors, and exceptional work separately.
- Recommend cheaper alternatives when they solve the actual job.

## Do Not Say

- UAE-standard/fair-market/proven prices.
- Guaranteed savings, revenue, SEO, conversion, reliability, uptime, or ROI.
- Universal POS/delivery integration.
- All-inclusive while vendors/VAT are excluded.

## Required Sales Assets

- Alternative-fit checklist.
- Owner discovery questionnaire and ROI calculator.
- Tier responsibility matrix.
- Proposal with allowances, acceptance, dependencies, support, incidents, vendors, export, cancellation, and payment schedule.
- T2 paid-discovery statement of work.

## First Question For Run 003

How should the sales proposal and discovery flow present these internal pricing hypotheses while clearly qualifying alternatives, responsibilities, and non-guaranteed ROI?
