# Hosting And Responsibility Pricing Options

| Option | Setup | Recurring | Ownership | Provider burden | Decision |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|---|
| Provider-managed | Base setup | Managed plan mandatory | Provider controls deployment; restaurant pays vendors/pass-through | Monitoring, backups, restoration/vendor coordination within boundaries | Launch default |
| Client-owned T0/T1 | +AED 750/+AED 1,000 | Optional care AED 250/AED 450 | Restaurant owns accounts/billing | Product support only if purchased; troubleshooting may be harder | Proposed |
| Hybrid launch then transfer | Managed launch; clean transfer AED 750-1,500 | Managed until transfer | Transfers after checklist | Export/credentials only; broader migration quoted | Conditional |
| Client-owned T2 | Not available initially | Later hypothesis AED 900/month | Restaurant-owned | Responsibility reduction unproven | Deferred |

## Responsibility Matrix

| Responsibility | Managed plan | Client-owned | Restaurant |
|---|---|---|---|
| Deployment/monitoring/backups | Bounded provider responsibility | Restaurant/vendor, unless quoted | Supplies access |
| Restoration/security coordination | Normal-hours bounded coordination | Quote/support plan | Accepts vendor risk |
| Order monitoring/acknowledgement | Excluded | Excluded | Owns |
| Menu/availability/fulfilment/refunds | Excluded | Excluded | Owns |
| Vendor uptime/charges | Pass-through; no guarantee | Restaurant owns | Pays |
| After-hours response | Separate agreement | Separate agreement | Own fallback |
| Export/cancellation | Defined clean export | Defined clean export | Accepts process |
| Broader migration | Custom quote | Custom quote | Provides access/acceptance |
