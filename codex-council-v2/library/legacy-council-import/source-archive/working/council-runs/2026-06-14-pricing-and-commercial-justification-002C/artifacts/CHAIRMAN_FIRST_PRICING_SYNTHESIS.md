# Chairman First Pricing Synthesis

**Status:** First synthesis for response round, not final verdict. All numbers are internal scenario assumptions in AED, excluding VAT and third-party costs.

## Emerging Structure

Setup fee + responsibility-based recurring service + transparent third-party costs + paid exceptional work. Discount launch setup only. Fixed prices apply only to bounded qualified scopes.

## Proposed Base Pricing

| Package | Launch setup | Standard setup | Managed monthly | Client-owned product support |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| T0 Ordering Page | 2,900 | 3,600 | 350 mandatory when managed | 250 optional |
| T1 Website + Ordering | 5,900 | 7,500 | 650 mandatory when managed | 450 optional |
| T2 Direct Order Workflow | 12,000 | 15,000 | 1,100 mandatory | 900 mandatory |

T2 moves to paid discovery/custom quote wherever custom workflows, integrations, advanced roles, unclear responsibilities, or high-variance operations appear. The later Evidence Auditor rejected a numeric hour threshold as fake precision.

## Hosting

| Mode | Setup adjustment | Recurring |
|---|---:|---|
| Provider-managed | Included | Managed plan mandatory |
| Client-owned T0 | +750 | Optional support after acceptance |
| Client-owned T1 | +1,000 | Optional unless provider retains responsibility |
| Client-owned T2 | +1,500 | Mandatory support |
| Later migration | AED 750-1,500 or quote | Defined export/transfer/exit scope |

Managed service includes bounded monitoring, backups, restoration coordination, security maintenance, and vendor coordination. It excludes restaurant operations, after-hours response, vendor charges, and unlimited changes.

## Allowances And Overages

- Initial menu items: T0 60; T1 100; T2 150.
- Revisions: T0 one; T1/T2 two.
- Training: T0 30 minutes; T1 60 minutes; T2 two 60-minute sessions.
- Monthly managed content: T0 none; T1 30 minutes; T2 45 minutes.
- Monthly product support: T0 30 minutes; T1 60 minutes; T2 120 minutes.
- Exceptional work: AED 275/hour in 30-minute increments.
- Training/content session: AED 300/hour.
- Clean menu entry: AED 350 per 25-item block.
- Product defects do not consume allowance.
- No rollover or implied after-hours SLA.

## Payment Terms

- T0/T1: 60% deposit, 40% after acceptance.
- T2: 50% deposit, 30% after workflow sign-off, 20% after acceptance.
- Third-party costs paid directly or transparently passed through.
- No long installment plans or annual discounts before retention/support measurement.

## Cheap WordPress Position

Recommend cheap WordPress, a freelancer, WhatsApp, POS/SaaS, aggregators, or doing nothing when they solve the actual job. The proposed offer is justified only by structured ordering and explicit workflow/support responsibility, not design quality alone.

## Confirmed Direction

- Three base packages plus gated modules.
- Separate setup, recurring service, pass-through costs, and exceptional work.
- Provider-managed hosting as launch default.
- Mandatory recurring support for T2.
- Fixed pricing only for qualified finite scopes.
- No guaranteed ROI, aggregator replacement, universal integrations, or hidden vendor costs.

## Preserved Dissent

- T0/T1 managed monthly willingness to pay is unknown.
- T2 may be safer as discovery-priced until measured.
- Fixed messaging module prices may still be premature.
- The cost model relies on unmeasured hours and support consumption.
