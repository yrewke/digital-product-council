# Allowances And Overages

| Item | T0 | T1 | T2 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Menu items | 60 | 100 | 150 |
| Photos | Restaurant-supplied within menu allowance | Restaurant-supplied within allowance | Restaurant-supplied within allowance |
| Pages | 1 ordering page | Up to 5 template pages | Up to 5 plus workflow |
| Languages | 1 | 1 | 1 |
| Revisions | 1 round | 2 rounds | 2 rounds |
| Training | 30 min | 60 min | 2 x 60 min |
| Monthly support | 30 min managed | 60 min managed | 120 min managed |
| Monthly updates | None | 30 min managed | 45 min managed |

- Exceptional work: internal AED 275/hour hypothesis, 30-minute increments.
- Training/content session: internal AED 300/hour hypothesis.
- Clean menu entry: internal AED 350 per 25-item block.
- Translation, rewriting, photography, cleanup, integrations, and custom design are quoted.
- Product defects do not consume allowance.
- No rollover.
- Incidents are classified before billing; restaurant/vendor-caused diagnosis may be chargeable.
- Urgent/after-hours work requires a separate agreement; no implied availability.
