# Pricing Options Comparison

| Option | Clarity | Provider protection | Sales friction | Decision |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| One-time setup + optional support | High | Weak where responsibility continues | Low initially | Use only client-owned T0/T1 after acceptance |
| Setup + mandatory monthly support | Clear when responsibility visible | Strong | Medium-high | Use managed T0/T1 and all T2 |
| Lower setup + higher monthly managed service | Improves cash entry | Creates retention/collection risk | Medium | Rejected for now |
| Annual-first package | Predictable cash | Buyer commitment untested | High | Deferred |
| Launch setup discount + standard pricing | Buys validation data | Safe if setup-only and cohort bounded | Medium | Chosen |
