# Stage 1 Advisor Evidence Requests

The five advisors worked in sealed contexts. No advisor queried NotebookLM.

## Restaurant Owner

1. What measurable financial or customer-retention outcomes have comparable restaurants achieved by shifting suitable repeat-customer orders or interactions to an owned channel?
2. What objections, trust concerns, and purchase-stopping risks do restaurant owners report when evaluating standardized direct-channel products?
3. Which direct-channel use cases do restaurant owners naturally buy together, and which are usually viewed as separate or premature capabilities?

## Restaurant Operator

1. During normal and peak periods, who receives, confirms, prepares, checks, and hands off each proposed direct-channel order type, and what failures occur when staff are overloaded?
2. What staff actions, training, supervision, and recurring discipline are required to keep menus, availability, order status, customer communication, and handoff accurate?
3. What narrowly scoped live pilot would test operational reliability across a normal and peak period?

## Market Economics Analyst

1. For which restaurant segments and repeat-order use cases can an owned direct channel measurably improve restaurant economics versus marketplace ordering or no digital direct channel?
2. Which observable restaurant characteristics distinguish likely-fit restaurants from poor-fit restaurants for a standardized product without early delivery or POS integrations?
3. Which assumptions are load-bearing for restaurant payback, and under what evidence-backed conditions does the economic case fail?

## Competitor and Alternatives Analyst

1. What customer jobs and workflows are served by WhatsApp, cheap QR menus, agencies, and doing nothing, and where do their capabilities stop?
2. What are the comparable commercial and operational commitments of a standalone product versus ordering SaaS and POS ecosystems?
3. When aggregators remain discovery channels, under what conditions do customers migrate to direct ordering, stay on aggregators, or use both?

## Business Builder

1. What acquisition channels and sales motions produce repeatable customer acquisition for standardized restaurant direct-channel products?
2. What onboarding steps, support incidents, and recurring provider tasks remain after launch when delivery and POS integrations are excluded?
3. Which restaurant custom requests occur most often, affect retention, and should be add-ons, roadmap candidates, or rejected work?

## Librarian Consolidation Boundaries

- Restaurant-side value, measurable ROI, and segment fit.
- Fulfilment models, peak-period operations, and reliability.
- Competitors, alternatives, and aggregator coexistence.
- Provider repeatability, onboarding, support, and customization boundaries.
