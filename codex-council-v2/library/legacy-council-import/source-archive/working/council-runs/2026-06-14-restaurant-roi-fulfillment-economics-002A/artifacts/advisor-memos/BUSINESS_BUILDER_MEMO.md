# Advisor Memo: Business Builder

**Commercial question:** Under which fulfilment model does owned direct ordering create positive ROI versus alternatives?
**Verdict:** conditional
**Strongest finding:** The best product boundary is not "all of delivery"; it is a bounded direct-channel engine that lets the restaurant keep aggregators while capturing repeat orders.
**Evidence used:** Run 001 evidence on coexistence and provider repeatability; Run 002A scenario ladder.
**Critical assumption:** The provider can stay standardized and avoid custom service creep.
**Tradeoff:** More integrations may increase usefulness but can also destroy repeatability.
**Disagreement expected:** The operator and competitor analysts will argue over whether WhatsApp is enough or whether a website is required.
**Evidence request:** Willingness to pay by segment, plus support minutes per account.
