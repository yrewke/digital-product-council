# Run 2C Pricing Handoff

## Chosen Package Model

Price a hybrid model with three base packages:

1. Tier 0 - Ordering Page
2. Tier 1 - Website + Ordering
3. Tier 2 - Direct Order Workflow

There is no fixed Tier 3. Price commerce and integration capabilities as separately gated modules.

## Base Packages to Price

For each Tier 0/1/2, price:

- Standard setup and included data/content allowance
- Included revision and training allowance
- Launch/acceptance work
- Recurring support and maintenance allowance
- Hosting/domain/infrastructure posture
- Backup/export/cancellation/exit obligations
- Excess/exceptional work

## Modules to Price

- WhatsApp 1 handoff
- WhatsApp 2 deterministic bot
- Telegram 2 deterministic bot
- Catering/group enquiry
- Language/content allowance
- Customer notifications
- Payment
- Delivery zones/fees/minimums
- Table QR
- Promotions/coupons
- Deeper analytics/source tracking
- Supported POS integration
- Supported delivery integration
- Custom/delayed AI, multi-branch, reporting, and automation quotation rules

## Support Obligations to Price

- Business-hours product support allowance
- Additional training/retraining
- Menu/content updates beyond allowance
- Incident response and after-hours work
- Vendor escalation and integration maintenance
- Backup/recovery/export/migration/cancellation
- Managed operations only as separately staffed/custom scope

## Third-Party Pass-Through Costs

Domains, hosting, messaging/BSP, AI, gateway/dispute/refund, POS/API, delivery-provider, maps, analytics, SMS/OTP, storage, and other vendor costs must be paid directly or passed through transparently.

## Provider Cost Drivers

Acquisition hours, qualification, menu/content cleanup, setup, revisions, translation, training, support minutes, incidents, vendor escalations, custom requests, maintenance, continuity, cancellation/export, and collection/payment risk.

Use `PROVIDER_COST_SCORECARD.md` only as a preliminary measurement template. Do not price from its ordinal scores without explicit assumptions.

## Restaurant Value Drivers

Structured order capture, reduced measured manual leakage, clear owned destination, known pickup/fulfilment, high-ticket enquiry capture, source/outcome measurement, and bounded commerce controls.

## Competitor Alternatives to Compare

Manual WhatsApp/phone, static QR menu, agency website, ordering SaaS, POS ordering module, aggregators, and doing nothing.

## Pricing Questions Still Open

- What setup/recurring structure balances urgent cash and ongoing obligations?
- What allowances are included per package?
- What minimum recurring support is required?
- How should launch-validation work be priced and bounded?
- Which common modules receive fixed prices versus quotes?
- What gross-margin/provider-contribution threshold is required?
- How should client-owned versus provider-owned infrastructure affect price?
- What payment terms and cancellation/export fees are appropriate?

## Exact First Question for Run 2C

What setup fee, recurring support structure, included allowances, module prices, and custom-quote rules make Tier 0, Tier 1, and Tier 2 understandable to UAE restaurant owners while covering measured one-developer acquisition, onboarding, support, continuity, and third-party-risk costs?
