# Provider Cost Ledger

| Scope | Setup effort assumption | Monthly support assumption | Risk | Commercial gate |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|
| Tier 0 standardized | Low | Low | Menu/data quality and missed notifications | Fixed scope, one revision allowance, named owner |
| Tier 1 standardized | Low-medium | Low-medium | Content/translation/custom design requests | Template boundary and content allowance |
| Tier 2 workflow | Medium | Medium | Training, status discipline, incidents | Workflow sign-off, training allowance, support cap |
| Tier 3 commerce | Medium-high | Medium-high | Payment/refunds/zones/customer issues | Provider compatibility, recurring support, pass-through costs |
| WhatsApp/Telegram deterministic bot | Medium-high | Medium | Channel policy, conversation exceptions | Volume/data gate and finite intents |
| AI assistant | High/variable | High/variable | Hallucination, tokens, escalations | Delay/custom, budget and safety gate |
| POS/delivery integration | High/variable | High/variable | Vendor access and failure | Supported adapter or custom quote only |
| Multi-branch/advanced operations | Very high | Very high | Scope explosion and continuity | Delay/custom; separate staffed model |
