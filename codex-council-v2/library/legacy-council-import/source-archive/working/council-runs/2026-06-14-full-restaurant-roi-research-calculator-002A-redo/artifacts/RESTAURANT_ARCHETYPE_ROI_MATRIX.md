|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Neighborhood QSR | Strong | repeat shift, pickup, structured menu, zone control | premium trust story | website + WhatsApp handoff/bot | pickup + existing driver/outsourced | Medium | orders, AOV, repeat, radius, fees | High |
| Fast casual | Strong | pickup, repeat capture, AOV testing, social traffic | AI assistant early | website + WhatsApp | pickup + hybrid | Medium | channel mix, AOV, margin, traffic | High |

| Grill/kebab restaurant | Strong | repeat families, pickup, direct delivery, group orders | reservation value | website + WhatsApp | hybrid | Medium | family orders, radius, delivery costs | High |
| Seafood restaurant | Medium | credibility, menu clarity, catering/group orders | frequent repeat direct ordering | website + enquiry/WhatsApp | pickup + quoted delivery | High | ticket, freshness/refund risk | Medium |
| Mandi/madhbi/traditional | Strong | family trays, repeat, catering, direct delivery | table QR first | website + WhatsApp + optional Arabic bot | hybrid | Medium | tray AOV, group orders, zones | High |

| Restaurant with POS | Medium-strong if compatible | queue consolidation, duplicate-entry reduction | integration if closed/expensive | website + dashboard + supported POS | current fulfilment | High | POS name/API/fees, order volume | Conditional |
| Multi-branch small group | Strong potential, high burden | shared brand, direct data, repeat, reporting | tiny standalone page | website + branch selection + dashboard | branch-specific hybrid | High | branch economics, menus, operations | Later priority |

## Priority Verdict

Start with WhatsApp-heavy, family-tray/catering-led, neighborhood QSR, grill/mandi, weak-website-with-demand, and aggregator-heavy restaurants that can provide actual numbers. Avoid leading with fine dining, low-volume discovery-only concepts, or complex multi-branch integrations.
