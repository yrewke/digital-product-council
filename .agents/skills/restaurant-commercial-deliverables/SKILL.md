# Restaurant Commercial Deliverables

Produce restrained business artifacts from an approved verdict without upgrading assumptions into facts.

## Required Reads

Read `references/output-contracts.md` and `references/sales-claim-safety.md`. Use the matching asset templates.

## Preconditions

Require:
- Chairman verdict path
- evidence pack path
- Evidence Auditor output
- requested artifact type

Stop if the verdict is missing, critical evidence gates are unresolved, or pricing has not been explicitly approved.

## Workflow

1. Separate approved decisions from proposed, rejected, deferred, and unresolved items.
2. Map each material claim to its evidence classification and citation.
3. Separate internal strategy language from customer-facing language.
4. Use conditional language for scenario-dependent benefits and ranges.
5. Put assumptions, evidence, and unresolved validation into separate ledgers.
6. Run a final claim-safety check before emitting customer-facing material.

## Hard Gates

- Never convert estimates, vendor claims, anchors, hypotheses, or inferences into verified facts.
- Never state universal savings, guaranteed ROI, or unsupported market claims.
- Never generate a final price recommendation unless the Chairman approved one.
- During skill-authoring tests, produce only synthetic fixtures, not real commercial deliverables.
