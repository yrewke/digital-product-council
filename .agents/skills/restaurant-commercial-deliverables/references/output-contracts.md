# Output Contracts

- **Package ladder:** Boundaries, target segment, restaurant value, provider burden, exclusions, evidence state, approval state.
- **ROI report:** Inputs, formulas, base/bear scenarios, sensitivity, citations, assumptions, limitations.
- **Sales presentation:** Customer problem, conditional value, differentiation, proof, limitations, next validation step.
- **Ledgers:** One row per claim, assumption, or evidence item with stable IDs and traceability.

Keep internal strategy and customer-facing output separate.
