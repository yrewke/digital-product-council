# Decision Taxonomy

- **Confirmed:** Evidence and council support are sufficient for action.
- **Proposed:** Promising direction requiring stated validation.
- **Rejected:** Fails a core commercial test or has a stronger alternative.
- **Deferred:** Intentionally postponed with a trigger for reconsideration.
- **Unresolved:** Critical evidence or disagreement prevents a decision.

For every item, state rationale, dissent, evidence strength, and what would change the classification.
