# Council Roles

Thinkers: `restaurant-owner`, `restaurant-operator`, `market-economics-analyst`, `competitor-alternatives-analyst`, `business-builder`.

Organizers: `research-librarian`, `evidence-auditor`, `chairman`, `devils-advocate`.

Thinkers analyze independently and may request evidence. They do not orchestrate or write verdicts. Organizers do not impersonate thinkers. The Evidence Auditor does not vote. The Devil's Advocate appears only after an initial direction forms.
