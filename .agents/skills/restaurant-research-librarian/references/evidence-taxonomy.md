# Evidence Taxonomy

Classify every material claim as exactly one primary type:

- **Verified fact:** Directly supported by a reliable cited source.
- **Estimated range:** Derived range with method and uncertainty stated.
- **Vendor claim:** Supplier assertion not independently verified.
- **Scenario assumption:** Chosen input for modeling, not an observed fact.
- **Inference:** Reasoned conclusion from cited evidence.
- **Contradiction:** Credible sources or handoffs materially disagree.
- **Unresolved gap:** Required evidence is absent or insufficient.

Also label geography: `UAE`, `GCC`, `global`, or `generic SaaS`. Never silently generalize across them.
